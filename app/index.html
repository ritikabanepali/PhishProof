Check For Spam

Spam emails are unsolicited messages, often trying to sell products, steal information, or spread malware. Before clicking any links or downloading attachments, look for the listed red flags:

Steps to Check

1. Urgency or threats ("Act now!" or "Account suspended!")

2. Generic greetings ("Dear Sir/Madam" or "Dear User")

3. Poor grammar and spelling errors

4. Mismatched or suspicious sender addresses, requests for sensitive information

5. Offers that seem too good to be true
